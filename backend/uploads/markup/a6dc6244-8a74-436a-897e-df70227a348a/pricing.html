Pricing FAQ

Still unsure which plan fits? Here's what people usually ask.

Are these prices fixed?
They're starting points. After a short discovery call we scope the exact work and send a fixed, itemized quote — no surprises later.
Do you offer payment in installments?
Yes. Projects are typically split into milestones (e.g. 40% to start, 30% at design sign-off, 30% at launch).
What if my project doesn't fit a plan?
That's what Scale is for. We'll build a custom proposal around your goals, timeline and budget.
Can I upgrade later?
Absolutely — many clients start with a focused build and add e-commerce, growth retainers or care plans as they grow.
